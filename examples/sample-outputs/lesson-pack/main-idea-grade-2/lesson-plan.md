# Main Idea Lesson Plan

Grade: 2

Subject: ELA

Duration: 45 minutes

## Objective

Students will identify the main idea and two supporting details in a short informational passage.

## Materials

* Short informational passage
* Main idea organizer
* Exit ticket

## Lesson Flow

1. Warm up: Read a short paragraph and ask what it is mostly about.
2. Model: Think aloud to separate interesting details from the main idea.
3. Guided practice: Complete one organizer together.
4. Partner practice: Students identify a main idea and two details.
5. Independent check: Students complete the exit ticket.

## Differentiation

* Provide sentence frames for students who need language support.
* Offer a highlighted passage for students who benefit from reduced visual load.
* Invite extension by asking students to write a title that matches the main idea.
